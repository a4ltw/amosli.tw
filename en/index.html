Shih-kuo (Amos) Li

Kaohsiung, Taiwan · Project Coordinator · Open-source community contributor

A kid from Penghu who occasionally wanders off. Somehow ended up walking the full Camino de Santiago and the Inca Trail, and lived for stretches on the U.S. East Coast, in France, and in Chile along the way. I like learning languages as a way of watching the world.

I see open-source culture as one possible answer to the harder problems in democratic politics — currently active in Cofacts fact-checking and lurking across g0v project channels. I care about open government, open data, digital literacy, and digital rights.

Formerly Project Coordinator at Open Culture Foundation (OCF), coordinating projects with Taiwan's Ministry of Digital Affairs, local governments, and international partners. Currently a board director at Software Liberty Association of Taiwan (SLAT), and volunteer track coordinator for COSCUP's open-source policy track for three consecutive years.

Also, I'm a veteran.
